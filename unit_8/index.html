About

Hey, my name is Anne-Laure!

I grew up around the world, mainly in hot places, on the African continent & Pacific Islands. Before becoming the mum of a little boy in 2015, I worked in the travel industry- which is lucky as I quite like travelling! I am also incredibly blessed to be married to the most amazing being I know.

To sum up, I am a traveller, a wife, a mum, a "lost in transition" triathlete (in reference to my horrendously long transitions during triathlon), a great outdoors fan, a part-time hippie, a craft-lover not-so-extraordinaire, born French, German-educated. I am now a London girl where we live with our dog Chilli Pepper and two cats Gin & Tonic.

This portfolio reflects what I love most in life.
